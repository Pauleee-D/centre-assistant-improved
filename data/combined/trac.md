### Insurance/ Rehabilitation & Funded Membership

Everybody is welcome at Thomastown Recreation & Aquatic Centre. TRAC is committed to making sure access to insurance and rehabilitation memberships is a simple and easy process for all. TRAC works with many insurers to streamline the application and payment system for you, so once you have your insurance claim approved, you can join and start your rehabilitation activities.

Your insurance and rehabilitation membership can be used for a variety of activities such as warm water physical therapy, lap swimming, gym work and exercise physiologists. Speak to your health professional about what therapy you require. TRAC work with many insurance agencies including; the Transport Accident Commission (TAC), Gallagher Bassett, GIO, EML, and Workcover, just to name a few.

Simply complete the enquiry form below to start this process, and make an Insurance Claim at TRAC.

##### How Our Exercise Physiologists Can Help

Thomastown Recreation & Aquatic Centre also have exercise physiologists to assist you with your recovery. Some memberships covered by WorkCover or TAC may also include approval for Exercise Physiology. With Accredited Exercise Physiologists on site we can set you up with a tailored rehabilitative program for you to use our state of the art gym or warm water therapy pool. With the combination of your membership and guidance from our health professionals, we can get you back on track and support you through your health and fitness journey.

For more information on our Exercise Physiology program, [click here.](https://www.trac.com.au/allied-health/exercise-physiology/)

**_*Please note as of July 1 their will be a CPI price increase._**

#### **Step 1**

Click the button to download your quote for your insurance company. You need to send this to your insurance company with your other documentation in order to receive their Approval letter.

[Insurance Quote](https://www.trac.com.au/wp-content/uploads/sites/82/2025/08/TRAC-Workcover-Insurance-Memberships-1.pdf-1.pdf)

#### Step 2 

AFTER you have received your approval letter from your insurer / funding agency, please email to trac@activewhittlesea.com.au with your full name, date of birth, phone number, emergency contact name and phone number, case manager name and contact details.

#### Step 3

Someone from our team will be in touch to get your membership started! We take care of all of the invoicing and following up payment to make the process easy for you.

### What happens next?

Once we’ve received your approval letter from your insurer / funding agency, we’ll be in touch with you in regards to:

  * establishing your membership with TRAC,
  * getting your account set up on Active World,
  * logging in and using the Active World app,
  * booking you in for your first BEGIN appointment



#### BEGIN with TRAC

Every new member at TRAC is entitled to three (3) FREE BEGIN sessions. These are sessions designed to get people started with their fitness goals.  
  
**Beginning Consultation – (Day 1-3)**

  * Pre-Exercise Screening

**Check in – (Day 14-17)**

  * Review Program, modify as required

# GOswim Swimming and Water Safety Program

## GOswim Swimming and Water Safety Program

#### Learn to swim with us!

Thomastown Recreation & Aquatic Centre is proud to deliver GOswim Swimming Lessons to students, families and the community in Thomastown and surrounding areas.  
  
GOswim lessons will equip students of all ages and abilities with essential swimming and water safety skills, focusing on five key learning areas:

  * Safe entries and exits 
  * Balance, movement and recovery 
  * Breath control, submersion and readiness 
  * Water safety 
  * Swimming stroke development



Enquire Now

### GOswim Lessons at Thomastown Recreation & Aquatic Centre

  * **Ages:** infants from 6 months of age through to adults
  * **Program runs for:** 48 Weeks
  * **Held in our:** indoor learn to swim pool and 25m pool
  * Ongoing Membership with **exclusive benefits!**
  * Students can join a **small group class** or request **private one-on-one lessons**



### GOswim Program

Developed in partnership with **Swimming Australia** , GOswim swimming and water safety lessons focus on contemporary and activity-based aquatic learning. GOswim is a fun and engaging way for everyone to learn to swim and stay safe in aquatic environments. Lessons empower participants to build confidence and improve swimming ability through exercises tailored to promote optimal development and competency.   
  
Students can progress at any time and will be awarded a medal upon level progression. 

### Overview of Learning Outcomes

  * Infants (6+ months)
  * Preschool (3+ years)
  * Children
  * GOswim Academy
  * Teens
  * Adults
  * All Abilities



#### Infants (6+ months)

Begin your child’s water safety journey from 6 months with GOswim sessions focused on water familiarisation. Parents or carers join their infants in the pool to learn essential skills in a fun, supportive environment.

  * Suitable for babies aged 6+ months (recommended to secure your enrolment from 5 months of age).
  * A Parent/Carer must be in the water with their infant for the entire lesson.



#### Preschool (3+ years)

Help your child build water confidence and independence. Lessons for preschool-aged children use fun, activity-based learning to teach safe entries and exits, breath control, and essential water safety skills.

  * Suitable for children aged 3+ years.
  * A parent or carer observes from poolside for the full lesson.



#### Children

Lessons for primary-aged children use a fun-filled and activity-based style of learning, focusing on the five key areas of learning:

  * Safe entries and exits 
  * Breath Control, Submersion and Readiness 
  * Balance, Movement and Recovery 
  * Stroke Development 
  * Water Safety 



#### GOswim Academy

GOswim Academy bridges the gap between learning swimming foundations and joining recreational or competitive squads. It encourages ongoing participation through school competitions, fitness swimming, and pathways into aquatic sports.

_GOswim Academy session availability may vary, please enquire to learn more._

#### Teens (13 – 17 years)

Help teens develop water safety awareness, build confidence, and improve swimming skills in a fun, social environment. Lessons are designed for teenage students and focus on essential skills, stroke development, fitness, and teamwork.

  * **TeenStart:** For beginners, building confidence and essential water safety skills. 
  * **TeenSkill:** For intermediate swimmers looking to improve stroke technique and endurance. 
  * **TeenActive:** For competent and confident swimmers looking to refine techniques, build fitness, and explore aquatic career pathways. 



__Teen lesson availability may vary, please enquire to learn more.__

#### Adults (18+ years)

It’s never too late to learn to swim or refine your technique. Develop water safety awareness, build confidence, improve swimming skills, and stay active in a calm, supportive environment. Lessons are designed for adult students (18+ years) and focus on essential skills, stroke development and fitness.

  * **AdultStart:** For beginners learning foundational skills like floating and breath control.
  * **AdultSkill:** For intermediate swimmers looking to improve stroke technique, breathing, and strength.
  * **AdultActive:** For competent and confident swimmers focusing on fun, fitness, endurance, and stroke efficiency.



_Adult lesson availability may vary, please enquire to learn more._

# About Us

## Our Story

Thomastown Recreation & Aquatic Centre is located in Thomastown. Our facility consists of Gym, Pool, Group Exercise Classes, Reformer Pilates, Women’s Only Gym .

We offer group exercise classes, which also include services for seniors, kids’ birthday parties and a small kiosk, open seven days a week. We are the perfect place for our community to make new friends while working on your health and fitness.

Come in and visit us today! We look forward to providing you with the best health and fitness experiences possible.

### Our Facilities

#### Health Club

We offer a wide variety of health and fitness facilities including a fully equipped gym, group exercise classes, personal training, Reformer Pilates and more.

[Learn More](/gym)

#### Aquatic

Our aquatic facilities feature 2 indoor heated pools, hydrotherapy pool to support recovery as well as a splash park for the little ones to enjoy.

[Learn More](/swim/)

#### Sports

Get moving in our court stadium set with basketball and netball hoops, futsal nets as well as volleyball, pickleball and upon request or social events.

[Learn More](/sports/)

GOswim swimming and water safety lessons are a fun and engaging way for kids and adults to learn to swim and be safe in aquatic environments. Suitable for infants from 6 months of age through to adults and offered all year round.

#### Birthday Parties

# Employment

## Work with us at Thomastown Recreation & Aquatic Centre

Our employees have access to an extensive range of career options, family-friendly workplaces and the chance to make a difference in their community.  
  
Our programs are designed to encourage everyone in our community to get active in any way they can and help people overcome their barriers with participation.
