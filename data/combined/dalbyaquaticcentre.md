### GOswim Program

Developed in partnership with **Swimming Australia** , GOswim swimming and water safety lessons focus on contemporary and activity-based aquatic learning. GOswim is a fun and engaging way for everyone to learn to swim and stay safe in aquatic environments. Lessons empower participants to build confidence and improve swimming ability through exercises tailored to promote optimal development and competency.   
  
Students can progress at any time and will be awarded a medal upon level progression.
