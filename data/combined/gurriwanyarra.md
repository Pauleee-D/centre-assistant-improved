## Welcome to the Gurri Wanyarra Wellbeing Centre

The Gurri Wanyarra Wellbeing Centre is Bendigo's premier aquatic & wellness centre, located on Browning Street, Kangaroo Flat.  
  
Staffed by a team of health, fitness & aquatics professionals, the centre provides a range of facilities, services & programs to cater for all members of the Bendigo community including:

  * Fully equipped 560 square metre Health Club
  * Group Exercise & Wellness Studios
  * Reformer Pilates Studio
  * Bendigo's newest indoor Olympic-sized pool (50m)
  * Warm Water Wellness Pool
  * Spa, Sauna & Steam Room
  * Swim School catering for infants through to adults
  * Bendigio's largest indoor zero depth Splash Park
  * Multi-Purpose Room for Community Bookings
  * Cafe & Birthday Parties
  * Exercise Physiology 
  * Personal Training 
  * Swimming Club (Kangaroo Flat SC)

##  Frequently Asked Questions Expand all 

#####  Does the App work on iPhones and Android? 

Yes, it sure does! You can download the Active World App from the App Store or the Google Store.

[Download App ](http://qrco.de/bbRx0F)

If you are using a different device, you can login to your portal view the web link here https://blau.activeworld.com.au/

#####  I am creating an account, but it says an account already exists. 

If you are already a member with us, we have set you up using the email you provided us upon joining – all you have to do is reset your password!

This means once you reset your password and login all your membership information will be ready for you to check!

#####  How do I book into a GOswim class? 

To book yourself or your child into a GOswim lesson all you need to do is select BookWeekly Lesson from your apps home screen. Select or add the member you wish to book into a GOswim lesson. If you or your child is already in a class, it will show you available classes to book a make up lesson. If you are not a current member of GOswim, you will be asked some questions on your swim ability then recommended a class to enrol into. At this stage you will need to buy a new membership to book the class. The app will take you through the process.

#####  How do I book into an Aqua Aerobics Class? 

To book yourself into an Aqua Aerobics session all you need to do is select Book Group Fitness from your apps home screen. Select the correct date and time in which you wish to attend a class, select the class you wish to attend and click continue. You will then be asked to select the person you wish to book into that class. If your membership includes access to classes, you will be able to see all classes available and book in. If you are not current member, you will need to go back a step and BUY a membership to book a class.

#####  My children’s memberships are not showing in my account. 

If you have multiple memberships with us in the centre – this could be yourself and your children, you should be able to see them within your account. If you cannot, please talk to our team next time you are in the centre or message us on the app to check the email address you have used to login is the primary email on all your memberships. If your child is not currently a member, you can sign them up in the app.

#####  Can I add my partner or a relative to my account? 

Yes, you can! If you would like to share your child's GOswim or other program progress with a family member, you can add them as a relative. From the home screen of the app, click on the 3 lines in the top left-hand corner (next to our centre logo). This will bring up the task bar on the left of your screen. Now select “MY RELATIVES” - you are able to view, add or remove relatives. Once added to your profile, the relative can download the app onto their phone and login using the same information on the relative profile (the email address you used). They will be able to see everything except personal details and account information

#####  How do I contact the centre on the app? 

We have made sure that we are only the click of a button away should you need to contact us! All you have to do is select “MESSENGER” from the home screen or the task bar to chat with our team.

#####  How do I update my billing details? 

We understand billing details need to be easy to update and change! So, we have made it simple! From the home screen of the app, click on the 3 lines in the top left-hand corner (next to our centre logo). This will bring up the task bar on the left of your screen. Select “MY BILLING DETAILS” scroll down to 'My Billing Details'. Select “EDIT” at the top of any section you wish to update your details in – don't forget to hit SAVE!

#####  What are ‘My Awards’? 

Awards are given when you or your child reach a milestone! Mostly used in our GOswim program, you or your child will be able to see all the achievements and what they can work towards!

#####  What is ‘Pathway’? 

The Pathway feature will give you a map view of how far you or your child have come in thier GOswim program! It will show you what levels are next and what learning outcomes need to be achieved to progress.

#####  My email address has changed, how do I update it? 

Login to your app and update your email address on your personal profile page.

#####  Is the app free? 

It sure is!

#####  I just changed my class day/time however the app has our original day/time listed. 

The App and Active World sync in live time. If your class day is showing incorrectly in your schedule, please contact the centre to ensure you are booked into the correct class.

#####  My App doesn’t show the update. 

Anyone with an IOS (Apple) you just need to update your device.

If you don't have auto update selected Go to APP Store -> Click Today (Bottom Left Hand Side) -> Click your profile pic (top right hand side) -> scroll down to active world app and click update.

  * booking you in for your first BEGIN appointment



## BEGIN with Gurri Wanyarra Wellbeing Centre

#### Every new member at GWWC is entitled to three (3) FREE BEGIN sessions. These are sessions designed to get people started with their fitness goals.  
  
Beginning Consultation - (Day 1-3)

  * Pre-Exercise Screening 
  * Goal setting
  * Program design and demonstration



#### Check in – (Day 14-17)

  * Review Program, modify as required 
  * Review experience at facility



#### Benefits consultation – (Day 30-35)

  * Member results are measured from initial appointment 
  * Set plan for following 30 days (including an additional check in if required)

Gurri Wanyarra also have exercise physiologists to assist you with your recovery. Some memberships covered by WorkCover or TAC may also include approval for Exercise Physiology. With the combination of your membership and guidance from our health professionals, we can get you back on track and support you through your health and fitness journey.

Classes are similar to using weights or machines in a gym, however, water aerobics give you a natural buoyancy to help protect and target particular areas of your body that require attention.   
  
Our professional group fitness instructors provide quality instructing to motivate you to work out to the best of your ability. With a wealth of knowledge and experience our group fitness instructors are sure to inspire you to achieve your fitness goals in a fun way!  


## The Benefits of Aqua Aerobics 

Water aerobics assists in the management of injuries and rehabilitation, allowing you to exercise the body without putting stress on your joints.  
  
Evidence supports the effectiveness of water aerobics for managing pain and stiffness, particularly back, hip, knee and shoulder pain. Including arthritis and other chronic and complex conditions which make it hard to move.

  * Burn calories
  * Build endurance
  * Increase flexibility
  * Reduce stress on joints
  * Increase muscle strength
