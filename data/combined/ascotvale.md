## AAA Swim and Social program

**Join into the Access All Abilities (AAA) Swim and Social program, engaging people with disabilities to enjoy water activities, followed by a chance to socialise afterwards and enjoy a tea or coffee.**  
  
The program is ongoing, with new timetables released every term.

#### About the program

Led by a qualified swim instructor, the program offers flexibility to choose from four engaging session types, including:  
  
**Water safety activities** \- Water introduction and familiarisation skill development  
**Aquacise** \- Aqua aerobics in water - low-impact exercise  
**Water sports** \- Beginner-level introductory water polo activities  
**Aqua Zumba** \- Aqua Zumba in-water dance exercise

#### Who is this for?

The Access All Abilities (AAA) Swim and Social program is open to all people with disabilities, aged 16 years and over.

#### Cost

$5.95 casual entry fee, or free if your existing MOVE membership contains aquatic access.  
  
Participants can attend sessions on a casual basis, allowing you to choose your preferred session type throughout the term.

KELC: 84 Quinn Grove, Keilor East 3033

#### Do I have to participate in each session?

No. You have the freedom to choose which sessions and locations you participate in! We recommend attending at least one session per week.

\- The Dance Society

Once you have your voucher, please present this to our reception team to process for your chosen program.

[Learn More](https://www.getactive.vic.gov.au/vouchers/)

### Get Active voucher FAQs

**What is Get Active Victoria?**

The Get Active kids voucher program is an initiative by the Victorian Government to help eligible families get their kids involved in organised sport and recreation activities by reimbursing the cost of membership and registration fees, uniforms and equipment. Eligible children may be able to receive from $50 up to $200 each.

**Who is eligible to claim the voucher?**

Parents can claim the rebate for children aged 0 to 18 and named on a valid health care card or pensioner concession card and medicare card to be eligible.  


**How long is the voucher valid?**

Each new Round of Get Active Vouchers will have an expiry date. Make sure you submit your voucher within the required claim period.

**What can the voucher be used for?**

The voucher can be used for any sporting program from an approved provider. We are an approved provider.

You can use this voucher towards the following programs at MOVE Moonee Valley:  
\- GOswim Swimming Lessons (AVLC & KELC)  
\- The Dance Society (KELC only)

**How do I claim the voucher?**

Step 1: Ensure you and your child meet the voucher criteria

Step 2: Enrol or Register into an eligible program offered at our Facility  
Step 3: Apply for a voucher (<https://www.getactive.vic.gov.au/vouchers/apply-for-vouchers/>)  
Step 4: Select Belgravia Health and Leisure as your provider OR Provider Name: BELGRAVIA HEALTH AND LEISURE GROUP PL and Provider Number: 2951161  
Step 5: Email or give your voucher to one of our friendly Customer Service staff members  
  
**Remember:**  
\- You can be reimbursed for an amount above $50 and up to $200 per eligible child. If your program registration cost is $100, you will only be reimbursed $100 not the total $200.  
\- You will need to complete a separate application for each child per rebate.  
\- You can only apply for 1 rebate per child per claim period.

#### Aquatic supervision

Parents/Guardians must actively supervise their children when in our venue. If you are not confident in the water or not a strong swimmer, please let our staff know as you enter the facility. 

[Learn More](https://www.movemv.com.au/swim/aquatic-supervision/)
