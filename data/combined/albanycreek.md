## Reformer Pilates

The Albany Creek Leisure Centre and Health Club is excited to introduce our brand new reformer Pilates studio!

Using spring loaded resistance on a reformer bed, our instructors will guide you through a variety of exercises designed to increase your strength, mobility, coordination and balance.

With 10 reformer beds and a wide selection of classes per week, reformer pilates is suited to all fitness levels and skills.

Book in via our Active World membership app up to 3 days in advance. See below for more information on downloading and using the Active World platform.

## The Playroom at Albany Creek Leisure Centre

The Playroom is designed to provide members with informal care, ADJUNCT CARE (Education and Care Services Act 2013 (ECS Act), of their children. Allowing you to enjoy the benefits of a childless work-out whilst our passionate and caring childcare team take good care of your little ones. We allow a maximum of 1 hour in care per child per day, please book to avoid disappointment.

All staff members are first aid trained and have obtained blue cards. The Playroom is open for children from 8 weeks old to 6 years of age. School children under the age of 12 years will be accepted during the school holidays.

## Cancellation of Booking

If a client cannot make their booking it is essential that they cancel via the ACTIVE World app or contacting the center before 7am on the day.

## Please Note - We are a Nut Free Zone!

This includes almond meal or any type of nut flour, almond milk, hummus, Nutella, banana bread with pecan or walnuts, and mortadella containing pistachios.

Please double check all products before putting them into your child's lunchbox to ensure no nuts are brought into the playroom.

## Welcome to Albany Creek Leisure Centre

Albany Creek Leisure Centre is proudly managed by Belgravia Leisure on behalf of the City of Moreton Bay.   
  
Our facility features a 50m outdoor pool, indoor heated pool, leisure pool with waterslide, and a fully equipped gym – all designed to support people of all ages and abilities.   
  
We’re proud to host a range of water sports and are home to the North Brisbane Polo Bears, Albany Creek Swim Club, and Masters Swimming Club. Whether you’re training, learning, or just enjoying the water, there’s something here for everyone.   
  
As one of North Brisbane’s largest health and fitness hubs, we offer year-round swimming lessons and group fitness programs in a welcoming, inclusive environment. Come and see what makes Albany Creek Leisure Centre a place the whole community can enjoy.

# About Us

## ******Our Story******

Albany Creek Leisure Centre is located in Albany Creek, QLD. We are proudly managed by Belgravia Leisure on behalf of the City of Moreton Bay.   
  


Our recently updated gym is fully air-conditioned for year round training. We offer a range of group exercise classes which also include services for seniors, kids’ birthday parties and a cafe with terrace overlooking the pool, open seven days a week. 

We are the perfect place for our community to make new friends while working on your health and fitness.

Come in and visit us today! We look forward to providing you with the best health and fitness experiences possible.

### Our Facilities

#### Health Club

We offer a wide variety of health and fitness facilities including a fully equipped gym, group exercise classes, Personal Training, Reformer Pilates & small group functional training. 

[Learn More](/gym)

#### Aquatic

Our aquatic facilities feature three heated pools, an outdoor 50m pool, indoor heated pool to support recovery as well as a splash park for the little ones to enjoy.

[Learn More](/swim/)

#### Swimming Lessons

GOswim swimming and water safety lessons are a fun and engaging way for kids and adults to learn to swim and be safe in aquatic environments. Suitable for infants from 6 months of age through to adults and offered all year round.

[Learn More](/learn-to-swim/goswim-swimming-lessons/)

#### Group Exercise

Group Exercise classes are a great social way to help build your confidence, motivation and accountability whilst making you smile. Our timetable features classes ranging from strength-based through to wellness.

[Learn More](/gym/group-exercise/)

#### Café

After building up an appetite in the gym or swimming pool, be sure to visit KT’s Café offering a range of snacks, coffee, drinks and food. 

[Learn More](/venue/cafe/)

#### Birthday Parties

Host your next birthday party with us, the only thing you need to do is send the invitations and bring the kids. We will organise everything and even clean up all the mess so you can enjoy a stress-free party with your children.

[Learn More](/venue/birthday-parties/)

#### Playroom

Our playroom is designed to assist parents and guardians getting active in our venue with peace of mind knowing that your child is being taken care of in a safe, supportive, well equipped environment.

[Learn More](/venue/childcare/)

#### Accessibility

We aim to provide a welcoming and comfortable environment for people of all abilities. Our facilities, programs and services have been designed to allow people of all abilities to lead active and healthy lifestyles.

[Learn More](/venue/accessibility/)
