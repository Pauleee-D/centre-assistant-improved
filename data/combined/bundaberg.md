# About Us

## Our Story

Bundaberg Aquatic Centre is a Bundaberg Regional Council facility located in Bundaberg West, Queensland. This new facility will bring a World Aquatics compliant 50m pool, two 25m pools and Reformer Pilates studio to Bundaberg and serve our community well into the next generations.

Set to open Spring 2025, Bundaberg Aquatic Centre will provide year-round access to state-of-the-art, health and wellbeing facilities and programs, like group exercise classes or swimming lessons.

We will be the perfect place for our community to make new friends while working on your health and wellbeing.

We look forward to providing you with the best experiences possible.

### GOswim Program

Developed in partnership with Swimming Australia, GOswim swimming and water safety lessons focus on contemporary, experiential, activity-based aquatic learning experiences. GOswim is a fun and engaging way for kids and adults to learn to swim and be safe in aquatic environments. Suitable for infants from 6 months of age through to adults, GOswim swimming lessons empower students to grow their swimming ability and confidence through a variety of exercises tailored to promote optimal development and competency.  
  
The GOswim Program at Bundaberg Aquatic Centre runs for 48 Weeks throughout the year, with a 4-week break over the summer holidays, allowing you to secure your spot all year long and enjoy our exclusive GOswim member benefits.   
  
Students can progress at any time and will be awarded a medal upon level progression.

### Overview of Learning Outcomes

  * Infants (6+ months)
  * Preschool (3+ years)
  * Children
  * GOswim Academy
  * Adults
  * All Abilities



#### Infants

Begin your child‚Äôs water safety journey from 6 months of age with GOswim sessions focusing on water familiarisation. Parents or Carers and their infants will learn essential swimming skills while enjoying themselves in a fun and comfortable environment. 

  * Suitable for babies aged 6+ months (recommended to make a booking from 5 months of age).
  * A Parent/Carer is required to be in the water with their infant for the full duration of the lesson.



#### Preschool

Ensure your little one is safe in and around water. Lessons for pre-school aged children implement a fun-filled and activity-based style of learning, focusing on safe entries and exits and breath control, helping to build independence and water confidence. 

  * Suitable for children aged 3+ years.
  * Parent/Carer observing from poolside for the full duration of the lesson.



#### Children

Lessons for primary-aged children implement a fun-filled and activity-based style of learning, focusing on the five key areas of learning:

  * Safe entries and exits 
  * Breath Control, Submersion and Readiness 
  * Balance, Movement and Recovery 
  * Stroke Development 
  * Water Safety 



#### GOswim Academy

Part of the GOswim Swimming and Water Safety Program Pathway, the GOswim Academy Levels 1 and 2 serve as a transition from learning the foundations of swimming and water safety to participation in recreation or competitive swimming squads. The GOswim Academy is designed to encourage continued participation in aquatic activity including involvement in competitive swimming, school competitions and also swimming for health and wellbeing. 

[Learn more about GOswim Academy](https://bundabergaquaticcentre.com.au/learn-to-swim/goswim-academy/)

#### Adults

It‚Äôs never too late to learn to swim or improve your existing swimming skills. GOswim swimming and water safety lessons are available for teens aged from 16 years through to adults. 

Lessons may be held in private or small group sessions. Various levels and class options are available to help you build or improve your confidence in and around the water.

# Reformer Pilates

## Practice strength and flexibility with Reformer

Reformer Pilates is a whole-body workout. Let our expert instructors guide you to improve your wellbeing in a supportive welcoming environment. 

  * Dedicated Reformer Pilates studio for intimate classes
  * Suitable for all levels of experience
  * Adaptable to various fitness levels by easing or increasing resistance
  * Low impact, joint-friendly workouts
  * Qualified instructors



###### Our timetable

With Reformer Pilates classes each week, there‚Äôs always a session to fit your schedule. Enjoy unlimited access as a member and explore our full timetable to find your perfect class!

### Benefits of Reformer Pilates

**Improve Core Strength**

Reformer Pilates focuses heavily on engaging the core muscles, including abdominals, lower back and pelvic floor. Strengthening these muscles improves posture, stability and overall functional movement.

**Enhance Flexibility**

Regular practice on the reformer helps improve both static and dynamic flexibility. The machine allows for controlled stretching and lengthening of muscles, promoting joint mobility and reducing stiffness.

**Better Posture**

Through targeted exercises, Reformer Pilates helps align the spine and strengthen postural muscles. This results in improved posture, which can alleviate back, neck and shoulder pain often caused by poor alignment. 

**Strengthen and Tone Muscles**

The resistance provided by the reformer machine allows for a full-body workout that targets all major muscle groups. It leads to increased muscle tone, strength and endurance ‚Äì especially in the legs, arms and core.

**Low Impact Workout**

Unlike high-impact exercise, Reformer Pilates provides a low-impact workout that reduces stress on the joints while effectively strengthening and toning muscles. This makes it suitable for people with joint issues or those recovering from injury.

**Improve Balance and Coordination**

The reformer machine encourages controlled movements that challenge your balance and awareness for self. This helps improve coordination, stability and overall movement efficiency.

**Injury Rehab and Prevention**

Reformer Pilates is often used for rehabilitation because it strengthens muscles in a safe and controlled manner, helping to prevent future injuries. The equipment can also be adapted for a range of abilities and injury concerns.

**Practice Mindfulness**

Reformer Pilates encourages us to slow down, concentrate on our breath and move fluidly through positions. This practice reduces stress and encourages us to find calm and mental clarity.
