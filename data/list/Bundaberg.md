## Companion Card

The Companion Card will require you to pay only for your entry. A second entry ticket will be issued for your companion at no charge. Present your Companion Card when you are paying your entry fee.

Concession entry fee for the holder of the Carer Card at all times. Present your Carer Card when you are paying your entry fee. It's our way of supporting and appreciating the incredible work carers do every day.
