## Parents and Babies Aquatic Fitness

Our newest aquatics program, PABA Fit is at Ferny Hills Aquatic Centre. Targeted at fitness and to introduce babies to the water. This 30-minute class provides excellent bonding and social opportunities for new parents, and is paced to suit different levels of ability.
