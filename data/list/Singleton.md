## Personal Training to Achieve Your Goals

Here at Singleton Gym &amp; Swim we have a range of personal trainers who specialise in an array of different areas including general fitness, weight loss, wellness, functional training, body building, rehabilitation and injury rehabilitation.

Personal training is a premium fitness service that provides the most innovative and varied fitness offerings. Members can receive coaching from one of our fully trained and qualified personal trainers at any time.

You can purchase and book in Personal Training sessions at reception. Or if you have any questions regarding these sessions, click ENQUIRE NOW or contact our Centre on 02 6572 1359 or email singletongs@belgravialeisure.com.au to speak to our experienced team.

## Concession Discount, Carers &amp; Companions

Please note that for all concession or pension casual entries, a valid concession card must be presented at reception. If you are unsure if your concession card qualifies, please call the centre 0n 1300 850 197.

All children under the age of 10 must also be actively supervised at all times and cannot be left in the facility alone.

## Companion Card

The Companion Card will require you to pay only for your own entry. A second entry ticket will be issued for your companion at no charge.Present your Companion Card when you are paying your entry fee.

For more information about the Companion Card program www.companioncard.org.au
