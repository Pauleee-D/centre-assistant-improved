## Accessibility and Diversity

Our equipment includes aquatic wheelchairs, lift hoist with a range of sling sizes and a hydraulic change bed.

We have two disabled/family change rooms and we have disabled parking spaces close to the door of the centre. All on one level, we have wheelchair friendly access from the carpark through to the pool.

We welcome and encourage all members of the community to come and enjoy our friendly centre.
