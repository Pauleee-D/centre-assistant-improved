## Concession Discount, Carers &amp; Companions

Please note that for all concession or pension casual entries, a valid concession card must be presented at reception. lf you are unsure if your concession card qualifies, please call the centre on 1300 850 197.

All childrenunder the age of 10 must alsobe actively supervised at all times and cannot be left in the facility alone.

## Companion Card

The Companion Card will require you to pay only foryour own entry.A second entry ticket will be issued for your companion at no charge. Present your Companion Card when you are paying your entry fee.

For more information about the Companion Card program www.companioncard.org.au

## Carers Card

The Carers Card will require you to pay only for your own entry. A second entry ticket will be issued for your companion at no charge. Present your Carers Card when you are paying your entry fee.

For more information about the Carer Card program www.carercard.vic.gov.au
