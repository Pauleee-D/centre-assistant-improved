## Health club upgrade

Health ClubUpgrade-FAQs.docx

We'regettinganupgrade-WaterMarcBanyule

Health Club Upgrade Comms

EDM - https://www.watermarcbanyule.com.au/were-getting-an-upgrade/

When will I receive information about the health club upgrade?

All members will be emailed using their contact details saved in Active World on 4 November 2025.

What is happening?

In December, the WaterMarc health club is getting a fresh new look and a full brand-new suite of TechnoGym equipment. While the gym is being renovated and the equipment installed, it will be closed with no access to 24/7. There will be a pop-up gym and reciprocal rights access to other facilities in Banyule.

When is it happening?

Works commence in December. All of Level 1 will close for ONE DAY ONLY on December 1. From December 2, Full Access and Premium members will be able to use the pop-up gyms, the cycle studio and the functional training room**

**outside of class times and will be subject to closure for some works

What is happening with my membership?

All memberships will remain active while the gym upgrade takes place. There are many options to continue with your exercise routine including, pop-up gym spaces, reciprocal access at Macleod &amp; Ivanhoe as well as group fitness and aquatic facilities.

What about the 1-day closure?

All memberships will receive a credit for the 1-day closure on 1 December. This will be applied automatically; you don't need to do anything.

When will the gym re-open?

The gym will remain closed from December 1 - December 19. The gym will re-open to members on December 20.

Can I still access 24/7?

No, due to the temporary nature of the pop-up, 24/7 access will be disabled. This access will be reactivated when the gym opens on December 20.

How doI access otherBanyule facilities?

AllWaterMarc memberswill need topresent their membershipband at eitherMacleod Recreation or Ivanhoe Aquatic. Both facilities will require you to check-in with you contact and membership details.

Where will there be a pop-up gym?

Equipment will be distributed across the Wellbeing studio and Childcare room. Members will also have access to the Functional Training room outside of class times. The Functional Training room will be closed at some stages for painting works and equipment install.

Will the pop-up gym be staffed?

There will be gym instructors rostered as usual throughout the pop-up gym spaces. We will continue delivering appointments during this period also.

## Can I do personal training sessions?

Yes.Access will be available for all our usual services.However, please check with your trainer to confirm their availability over this period.

## Will there be Wellness classes running?

Yes! The group fitness timetable has had some location changes to ensure impact is minimised. The timetable will be published soon with minimal changes to class availability.

## Will I be able to use childcare during this period?

Our members who currently use the childcare space will receive a separate email with more information regarding this service. While the gym is closed, this space will be repurposed to house a temporary gym. For any members who need this service, we are offering access to Mill Park.

## How doI access childcareservices atMillPark?

All interested members whohave made a childcarebooking in thelast 6months will need tocomplete an interest via the link in their email or using the QR code in the Childcare foyer.

## What canI access at MillPark?

Members will be able to utilize Health Club, Aquatic Areas and Group Fitness with an active WaterMarc membership when they have a childcare booking.

## What days can I book MillPark?

Mill Park operates their childcare service on Monday, Tuesday &amp; Thursday every week. Rosa and Michele will be working there on these days, so all children will have a familiar face.
