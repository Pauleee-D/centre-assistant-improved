# Keilor East Leisure Centre

## Welcome to Keilor East Leisure Centre

Keilor East Leisure Centre is the newly-developed state-of-the-art leisure centre and is Moonee Valley’s largest capital works project ever, boasting an incredible facility that encompasses wellness and leisure in one location.  
  
Keilor East Leisure Centre hosts both indoor and outdoor pools, a fully-equipped gym, Group fitness, wellness and cycle studio, an indoor kids water playground, dedicated warm water program pool, spa, steam and sauna, cafe, childcare and much more. Become a member and get ready to enjoy the incredible facilities and programs!

# AAA Swim and Social program

**Available at** :

  * Keilor East Leisure Centre
  * Ascot Vale Leisure Centre



## AAA Swim and Social program

**Join into the Access All Abilities (AAA) Swim and Social program, engaging people with disabilities to enjoy water activities, followed by a chance to socialise afterwards and enjoy a tea or coffee.**  
  
The program is ongoing, with new timetables released every term.

#### About the program

Led by a qualified swim instructor, the program offers flexibility to choose from four engaging session types, including:  
  
**Water safety activities** \- Water introduction and familiarisation skill development  
**Aquacise** \- Aqua aerobics in water - low-impact exercise  
**Water sports** \- Beginner-level introductory water polo activities  
**Aqua Zumba** \- Aqua Zumba in-water dance exercise

#### Who is this for?

The Access All Abilities (AAA) Swim and Social program is open to all people with disabilities, aged 16 years and over.

#### Cost

$5.95 casual entry fee, or free if your existing MOVE membership contains aquatic access.  
  
Participants can attend sessions on a casual basis, allowing you to choose your preferred session type throughout the term.

#### Where is the program held?

The AAA program is held at both Ascot Vale Leisure Centre, and Keilor East Leisure Centre to provide _flexibility_ and convenience.  
KELC: 84 Quinn Grove, Keilor East 3033  
AVLC: Cnr Epsom Road & Langs Road, Ascot Vale 3032

#### When are the sessions held?

Sessions are held on:  
**Tuesdays** from 1:30pm - 3:30pm at **Keilor East Leisure Centre** , and  
**Saturdays** from 1:30pm - 3:30pm at **Ascot Vale Leisure Centre**.

#### Do I have to participate in each session?

No. You have the freedom to choose which sessions and locations you participate in! We recommend attending at least one session per week.

  * Aqua



##### 45 minutes | medium intensity

Aqua is low-impact, high-energy workouts conducted in water. Participants perform a variety of aerobic, strength, and flexibility exercises. The water provides resistance and reduces stress on the joints, making it suitable for all levels. Benefits include improved cardiovascular health, muscle strength, flexibility, balance, and coordination. Certified instructors lead the classes, which are accompanied by music for a fun and motivating experience.

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Aqua Warm



##### 45 minutes | low intensity

These classes can help relieve muscular aches and pains, increase blood flow to muscles and joints, improve flexibility and promote relaxation. Perfect for rehab, treating injuries or relaxing. The buoyancy of the water supports the body, taking the weight off inflamed and painful joints. Aqua Warm is a fun gentle warm water class. Designed and catering for beginners and pre/post-natal. Classes are social, gentle and conducted in a comfortable and safe environment. This class is run in our Warm Water Pool. 

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Aqua HIIT



##### 45 minutes | medium - high intensity

Aqua HIIT is a high intensity interval training class that alternates short periods of intense exercise with less intense recovery periods. The buoyancy properties of the water allow you to work at a higher level with less impact on your body. This class is run in the 25-m pool.

Available at: Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Aqua Pilates



##### 45 minutes | medium intensity

Aqua Pilates is a gentle activity in the warm water pool that combines cardiovascular training, muscle strengthening, and breathing exercises. The aim of Pilates is to work all the stabilising muscles of the body, both deep and superficial, while putting the body in a state of imbalance.

Available at: Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Aqua Zumba



##### 45 minutes | medium - high intensity

Aqua Zumba is a dance based class that takes place in a pool. The philosophy was derived from the popularity of Zumba and adapted to water. The class comprises of low impact, high energy moves. An Aqua Zumba class is accompanied by Latin style, motivational music, including fast and slow rhythms, incorporating resistance training utilising water.

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Mat Pilates



##### 45 minutes | medium - high intensity

Is a form of exercise that focuses on strength, flexibility, and core stability. It is performed on a mat using bodyweight exercises and controlled movements to improve posture, alignment, and overall body strength. In Mat Pilates, participants follow a series of exercises that target the deep muscles of the core, including the abdominals, back, hips, and glutes. The movements are precise and controlled, emphasising breath control and proper alignment.

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Zumba



##### 45 minutes | medium intensity

Experience a dance fitness program that combines Latin and international music with dance movements and aerobic exercises. Zumba classes are known for their high-energy and infectious atmosphere. The workouts feature a variety of dance styles, including salsa, cumbia and more. Participants follow the instructor's lead and engage in choreographed dance routines.

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * Active Adults



##### 45 minutes | all levels

These classes that focus on getting every-BODY moving and will cater to those with arthritis or ailment that restrict range of movement. The classes build strength (we use the bands, light weights or bodyweight), balance, coordination and flexibility. Not only a great workout but loads of fun! We encourage everyone to move at their own pace and do what works for them. 

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

  * MIOLI (Move It Or Lose It)



##### 45 minutes | all levels

A low impact moderate impact class for over 50s. Features a combination of light cardio, strength and flexibility (stretching) exercises. 

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre

  * Circuit



##### 45 minutes | medium - high intensity

A circuit class incorporates a number of different exercises and workouts. These include cardiovascular, resistance and weight training. This is to ensure it gives you a balanced session and helps you reach your fitness targets as quickly as possible. Suitable for all fitness levels.

Available at: Ascot Vale Leisure Centre and Keilor East Leisure Centre

[Learn More](https://www.getactive.vic.gov.au/vouchers/)

### Get Active voucher FAQs

**What is Get Active Victoria?**

The Get Active kids voucher program is an initiative by the Victorian Government to help eligible families get their kids involved in organised sport and recreation activities by reimbursing the cost of membership and registration fees, uniforms and equipment. Eligible children may be able to receive from $50 up to $200 each.

**Who is eligible to claim the voucher?**

Parents can claim the rebate for children aged 0 to 18 and named on a valid health care card or pensioner concession card and medicare card to be eligible.  


**How long is the voucher valid?**

Each new Round of Get Active Vouchers will have an expiry date. Make sure you submit your voucher within the required claim period.

**What can the voucher be used for?**

The voucher can be used for any sporting program from an approved provider. We are an approved provider.

You can use this voucher towards the following programs at MOVE Moonee Valley:  
\- GOswim Swimming Lessons (AVLC & KELC)  
\- The Dance Society (KELC only)

**How do I claim the voucher?**

Step 1: Ensure you and your child meet the voucher criteria

Step 2: Enrol or Register into an eligible program offered at our Facility  
Step 3: Apply for a voucher (<https://www.getactive.vic.gov.au/vouchers/apply-for-vouchers/>)  
Step 4: Select Belgravia Health and Leisure as your provider OR Provider Name: BELGRAVIA HEALTH AND LEISURE GROUP PL and Provider Number: 2951161  
Step 5: Email or give your voucher to one of our friendly Customer Service staff members
