## CentrePoint Sport and Leisure Centre

**CentrePoint Sport and Leisure Centre** , located in Blayney, NSW, is a vibrant multi-activity facility managed in partnership with **Blayney Shire Council**. We offer a wide range of programs and activities designed for all ages and abilities.   
  
Our amenities include a 25-metre indoor pool, a dedicated programs pool, a splash pool, two fully equipped gym spaces, and a full-size indoor court suitable for netball, basketball, or your sport of choice—making it a welcoming space for the whole family to enjoy.

### Come and play!

Learn more about the sports currently being played at CentrePoint Sport and Leisure Centre.

  * [](https://centrepointblayney.com.au/sport/netball/)

#### Netball

Netball is a fast-paced team sport played on a court with a raised goal at each end. Teams consists of seven players, and the objective is to score goals by passing the ball down the court and shooting it through the opponent’s goal ring. Netball is a high-impact sport which requires speed, agility, and teamwork. Excellent for cardiovascular fitness, hand-eye coordination, and strategic thinking. 

[Learn More](https://centrepointblayney.com.au/sport/netball/)

  * [](https://centrepointblayney.com.au/sport/basketball/)

#### Basketball

Basketball is a high energy team sport played on a rectangular court with a hoop at each end. Each team consists of five players who work together to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent’s hoop.

[Learn More](https://centrepointblayney.com.au/sport/basketball/)

# Aquatic Facilities

## **Aquatic Facilities** at CentrePoint

CentrePoint Sport and Leisure offers a wide variety of aquatic facilities ensuring there is something for everyone to enjoy all year round. 

### Explore our Aquatic Facilities

#### 25m indoor pool

Our six-lane indoor heated 25-metre pool provides recreational swimmers and lap swimmers alike a place to dive in year-round.

#### Program pool

Specifically catering for beginner and intermediate swimmers, this dedicated pool will allow tuition to all ages and abilities making learning to swim a great experience.

#### Children’s splash pad

Our splash pad entertains little ones in the shallow water and features a water playground with waterfalls and slides.

#### Changerooms

All changerooms, including family changerooms, are conveniently located near pool deck for easy access before and after your swim. 

#### **Swimwear Policy**

Appropriate swimwear must be worn at all times to maintain a clean, safe and inviting environment for all our patrons. Clothing, other than swimwear, can create problems with water clarity, pool disinfectant and our water filtration systems.
