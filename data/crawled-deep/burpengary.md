## Welcome to Burpengary Regional Aquatic Leisure Centre

We are proudly managed by Belgravia Leisure on behalf of the City of Moreton Bay.   
  
Serving the Burpengary community and surrounding areas, our multi-activity facility offers inclusive access to swimming, fitness, and recreational programs for people of all ages and abilities.   
  
Our centre features a wide range of amenities for the whole family, including a 50-metre indoor pool, a 25-metre hydrotherapy pool, an outdoor mushroom pool, and our exciting new splash pad — now open!   
  
Stay active in our fully equipped gym, offering over 40 group exercise classes each week, plus access to personal trainers. We also offer GOswim lessons, squad swimming, tennis courts, BBQ facilities, and a welcoming on-site café.

# About Us

## ******Our Story******

Burpengary Regional Aquatic Leisure Centre is located in Burpengary, QLD. We are proudly managed by Belgravia Leisure on behalf of the City of Moreton Bay. 

Our facility consists of health club gym, pools, splash pad, tennis courts, cafe and BBQ facilities.

We offer group exercise classes and a gym open seven days a week. We are the perfect place for our community to make new friends while working on your health and fitness.

Come in and visit us today! We look forward to providing you with the best health and fitness experiences possible.

### Our Facilities

#### Health Club

We offer a wide variety of health and fitness facilities including a fully equipped gym, group exercise classes, personal training, and monthly member challenges.

[Learn More](/gym)

#### Aquatic

Our aquatic facilities feature 2 indoor heated pools, an outdoor mushroom pool, as well as a splash park for the little ones to enjoy.

[Learn More](/swim/)

#### Sports

Get moving on our two tennis courts. Bookings to be made at reception.

[Learn More](/sports/)

#### Swimming Lessons

GOswim swimming and water safety lessons are a fun and engaging way for kids and adults to learn to swim and be safe in aquatic environments. Suitable for infants from 6 months of age through to adults and offered all year round.

[Learn More](/learn-to-swim/goswim-swimming-lessons/)

#### Group Exercise

Group Exercise classes are a great social way to help build your confidence, motivation and accountability whilst making you smile. Our timetable features classes ranging from strength-based through to wellness.

[Learn More](/gym/group-exercise/)

#### Café 

After building up an appetite in the gym or swimming pool, be sure to visit our Café offering a range of healthy snacks, coffee and hot food options.

[Learn More](/venue/cafe/)

#### Accessibility

We aim to provide a welcoming and comfortable environment for people of all abilities. Our facilities, programs and services have been designed to allow people of all abilities to lead active and healthy lifestyles.

[Learn More](/venue/accessibility/)

#### Birthday Parties

Host your next birthday party with us, the only thing you need to do is send the invitations and bring the kids. We will organise everything and even clean up all the mess so you can enjoy a stress-free party with your children.

[Learn More](/venue/birthday-parties/)

#### Carnival Events

We can cater for school carnival swimming events. 

[Learn More](/venue/childcare/)
