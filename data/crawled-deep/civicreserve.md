## Welcome to Civic Reserve Recreation Centre

  
  
Located within the picturesque Civic Reserve Mornington, Civic Reserve Recreation Centre is an inclusive venue hosting a boutique gymnasium, group exercise studio, wellness classes, two court multi-sport stadium facility and a range of different services, including personal training and kiosk facilities.

The venue is also home of the Mornington Youth Club Gymnastics, Mornington Peninsula Table Tennis Association and Mornington Tennis Club.

This multi-activity regional venue serves the Mornington community and surrounding suburbs catering for the inclusion of people of all ages and abilities.

With something for everyone to enjoy, we look forward to providing you with our best health and leisure services possible.

### Active Recovery at Civic Reserve

Active Recovery is a fitness technique that uses light exercise—such as stretching, breathwork and low-intensity movement—to promote healing, reduce muscle soreness and enhance recovery after strenuous workouts. These 30-minute sessions may include self-massage, posture and core work and relaxation methods to improve blood flow, release tension and support overall body balance.

# Member Benefits

## 

## Become a Member

Become part of the Mornington Peninsula Leisure community with an exclusive membership giving you access to all 5 of our recreational centres. With memberships to suit all health and wellness goals. All full access memberships include a 3 stage personalised gym progrmming.  
  
It's easy to become a member with us, choose an option to suit your needs and join online or upon your first visit to the facility! Our memberships are powered by innovative technology, Active World. When you click the JOIN button you will be taken to the Active World portal to register as part of your joining process.  
  
If you need assistance our friendly staff can help you select the right membership option to help you achieve your results and find the best value for money.  
  
From the moment you walk into Civic Reserve Recreation Centre, you will feel comfortable, confident and full of energy. When you become a member with us, you'll not only receive a fantastic place to visit, exercise, relax and enjoy any day of the week but an entire lifestyle experience.

## Membership Benefits

When you become a member with us, you are committing to your health and overall well-being.  
  
Regular exercise can help reduce the risk of health concerns and diseases. It's also a great way to establish a healthy routine, become a part of a community of like-minded people and make new friends.  
  
Regualr exercise can also help reduce the risk of certain health conditions, including:

  * High blood pressure
  * Depression
  * Stress-related illness
  * High cholesterol
  * Heart disease 



## Establish a Routine and Challenge Yourself

Having a membership with us means you have access to centre facilities, so you can visit us at any time.  
  
Use this membership as a motivator to establish a regular fitness routine, create new healthy habits, meet like-minded people and invest in you overall health and happiness.

# Introductory Program

## Join our BEGIN Introductory Fitness Program

Getting started at a fitness club can be a daunting experience. We believe that every member deserves the right advice and ongoing support to achieve their health and fitness goals.   
  
To enhance our member experience, we offer immediate health and fitness advice upon becoming a member with us through the BEGIN fitness program.

  * Results are guarenteed
  * BEGIN is completely FREE to full access members
  * Best value services for our new and existing full access members
  * Ongoing support to achieve your goals and see results sooner
  * Look and feel your best with BEGIN



Become a full access member to receive the best start to your fitness journey with our BEGIN fitness program, to enquire please complete an enquiry form on our contact page. 

[Member Benefits](/join/member-benefits) [Memberships & Passes](/join/memberships-passes)




## What Can You Expect from BEGIN?

All new members receive three sessions in your first 30 days of joining our club. You'll meet with one of our highly qualified health professionals who will take you through the BEGIN program.   
  
Determining your current health and fitness ability and recommend a custom training program tailored to your individual needs.  
  
To ensure you don't get bored of your specialised fitness program we apply time efficient and effective techniques.  
  
Tailoring each member’s personal fitness plan by using the latest in scientific approaches, ensuring we create the perfect program for you!

  * A free program for all full access members.

  * Receive a tailored fitness plan using the latest in scientific methods.
  * Free online meal plans and nutritional information.

#####  Fitness Results are Guaranteed 

When it comes to your fitness you don't want to waste time or money, you want to see results!  
  
When members commence our BEGIN program results are guaranteed. We take the time to get to know our members, where they are currently at with their fitness goals and where they want to be. 

The BEGIN program will ensure you feel confident and competent in your ability to perform your workout. This will lead to guaranteed results and the best part is that it’s all free with your full access membership.   
  
We make sure you get off to the right start on your fitness journey. We work to keep you motivated all year round by offering regular progress checks and updates.

# Social Sports

## Social Sports on the Mornington Peninsula

**Come down to Civic Reserve Recreation Centre and Somerville Recreation Centre to play social Pickleball and Badminton! Bring your friends and family or try it on your own.  
  
Unlimited weekly play with a Mornington Peninsula Leisure membership, starting at $16.60 per week.**

Want to try it out? You can also pay for a casual social play session.

Cost: $9.30 (A) $7.70 (C) per session. Multi Visit Passes also available.
