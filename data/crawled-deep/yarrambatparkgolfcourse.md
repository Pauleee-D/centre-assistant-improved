## Welcome to Yarrambat Park Golf Course

Yarrambat Park Golf Course is located in the northern suburbs of Melbourne, only an hour from Melbourne’s CBD. This well renowned 18-hole golf course is open seven days.

Our golf facilities include the first Goanna Golf, a state-of-the-art 18 hole mini golf course to be enjoyed by the whole family, a 13-bay driving range with four undercover bays, pro shop, function centre, Grass Lands Café and bar, as well as access to PGA-qualified golf professional services.

If you're looking for a little extra practice, we have a free practice area with a putting and chipping green.

Whether you’re after a casual round of golf, perfecting your technique in a one-on-one session, looking for a fabulous event space with golf course views, or a bar to relax and unwind with friends – we have something for everyone.

# Teemates Junior Program

## Play Yarrambat For Less! 

### About

TeeMates is Golf Australia's new golf community which supports all kids on their golfing journeys, giving kids and teenagers awesome access to all types of golf all over Australia.

It’s fun and interactive, gives kids a chance to play golf with their peers, get a Golf Australia handicap which helps track rounds and progress, a membership gift, and the chance to get out and play events!

Everybody is welcome at TeeMates, and we just want kids to have fun and go play!  


### Benefits

Here are just a few great things TeeMates members get access too:

  * Play 9 holes at YPGC for just $5.00! (Weekdays & Weekends after 12PM)
  * Play golf for $5 through Youth on Course venues across Australia.

  * Get an official Golf Australia handicap which allows tracks your golf and progress.

  * A membership gift

  * Opportunities to play events specifically for kids and teenagers.

  * $30 local Tour registration credit with U.S. Kids Golf

At TeeMates you have a choice of three offerings to continue your journey in golf.

## 

## **Discover golf in a fun, supportive and social environment.**

The **Women’s Golf Pathway** is YPGC's golf program designed exclusively for women. Whether you're completely new to golf or looking to build your skills and confidence, the pathway offers a welcoming, no-pressure way to learn, play, and connect with others.

We’re a proud signatory of the **R &A Women in Golf Charter** and are a committed supporter of female golf participation.

### 

### Bronze

The Bronze level of the Women's Golf Pathway focuses on building basic skills, introducing golf etiquette, and helping you feel confident and comfortable both on and off the course. You'll also enjoy the chance to connect with other women who are starting their golf journey — because learning is always better together.

#### Level Inclusions

\- 8 Golf Clincs with our WPGA Professional.  
\- Driving range access (one small bucket per day).  
\- 10% discount on retail.  
\- Coffee or tea post clinic.  
  
  
  


###### All new participants start at bronze, after completing 4 terms you will proceed to silver. 

### 

### Silver

The Silver Level of the Women’s Golf Pathway is designed for women who have completed four terms at the Bronze level and are ready to take their game to the next stage. You’ve learned the basics — now it’s time to sharpen your skills, gain more course experience, and build confidence in all areas of play.

#### Level Inclusions

\- 8 Golf Clincs with our WPGA Professional.  
\- Driving range access (one small bucket per day).  
\- Golf couse access (Tue/Thur & Friday between 10am-2pm)  
\- 20% discount on your first set of golf clubs.  
\- 10% discount on retail.  
\- Coffee or tea post clinic.  


###### After completing 4 terms you will proceed to Gold* (The Gold level can only be accessed by YPGC Members).

### 

### Gold

The Gold Level of the Women’s Golf Pathway is designed for women who have completed four terms at the Silver level. At this stage, participants have a solid foundation in technique, rules, and course play — and are now focused on fine-tuning their performance through targeted coaching and more in-depth learning.

#### Level Inclusions

\- 8 Golf Clincs with our WPGA Professional.  
\- 30% discount on your first course membership.  
\- Driving range access (one small bucket per day).  
\- 20% discount on your first set of golf clubs.  
\- 10% discount on retail.  
\- Coffee or tea post clinic.

###### The Gold level can be accessed on an ongoing basis with a current YPGC course membership.

##  FAQ Expand all 

#####  What if a clinic is cancelled or I am away? 

If a clinic is cancelled due to weather or coach illness, we will absolutely reschedule it to ensure you receive all 8 sessions. 

#####  I’m going on holiday, can I make up the class I’ll miss? 

Absolutely. If you’re unable to attend your usual session due to holidays or other commitments, just let us know in advance. We’ll do our best to find a suitable make-up class or alternative session.

#####  Why do I need to pay upfront for the term? 

Upfront payment ensures fairness to our coaches and supports delivery of all the added value the program includes, such as post-clinic drinks, equipment access, range balls, and golf course access (silver level only).

#####  When can I progress to the next level? 

You’ll move through the Pathway based on term completion. After **four terms at Bronze** , you’ll move to Silver. After **four terms at Silver** , you’ll be eligible for Gold, where you'll continue learning as a full member or Lifestyle Access Pass holder.

# The Grass Lands

## 

**Step into the grazey world of laid-back chews and local brews**  
  
Whether you’ve just finished a round of golf, tackled a mini golf adventure, or you're simply cruising by Yan Yean Road, we’re here to serve up good times at _The Grass Lands_.

Relax in our beautiful bistro with a craft beer in hand, overlooking the beautifully striped 18th fairway of our championship golf course and the vibrant, nature-inspired Goanna Golf mini course — complete with native plants and whimsical animal sculptures.   
  
Looking for sunshine and good vibes? Our beautiful outdoor beer garden is the perfect spot to unwind with mates, sip on something cold, and graze the afternoon away.

### Chews & Brews

We’re a fully licensed venue open 7 days a week, with extended hours during daylight savings - perfect for those post-work drinks and long summer evenings. From our stunning 18-hole championship golf course, mini golf, driving range and practice greens, to our beautiful beer garden and bistro ideal for private functions, we’ve got everything you need in one scenic spot. Whether you're planning a casual catch-up or a special celebration, we've got the perfect setting for any occasion!

### Functions & Events

From our ever-popular Friday Night Fun sessions in summer to unforgettable private functions and weddings, our golf course is so much more than just a haven for golfers. Celebrate your milestone moments surrounded by stunning views and relaxed vibes, the perfect backdrop for birthdays, engagements, weddings, and everything in between. Our dedicated team is here to take care of all the details, so you can relax and enjoy every minute of your special event.

### Food & Beverage at Yarrambat Park Golf Course 

**Full Menu Available Daily – Monday to Sunday | 9:00am – 3:00pm**  
Whether you're fueling up before your round, relaxing after a game, or just stopping by to enjoy our scenic surrounds, we've got you covered with a great range of food and drink options. 

Join us for a delicious bite to eat any day of the week! Our kitchen is open daily from 9am to 3pm, offering a full dining menu packed with fresh, seasonal favourites. From hearty breakfasts and light snacks to satisfying lunches, there's something to suit every appetite. 

Whether you're dining solo, catching up with friends, or celebrating with a group, our relaxed clubhouse setting is the perfect spot to sit back and enjoy quality food in good company.   
  
**Beverages**

We’re fully licensed and proud to offer a great selection of drinks to suit every occasion. 

On Tap: Enjoy a cold beer, wine, or spirit from our clubhouse bar, operating under our restaurant liquor licence. 

On Course: Fancy a drink while you play? We also hold a limited licence allowing the sale of takeaway alcohol to enjoy responsibly on the course. 

From a morning coffee to an end-of-round brew, we’ve got the drinks flowing all day long. 

**Happy Hour**

Every Friday | 4:00pm – 6:00pm 

Kick off the weekend in style with our Friday Happy Hour! Grab your mates and enjoy great value drinks and good vibes every Friday from 4pm to 6pm. It’s the perfect way to unwind after work or wrap up a round of golf.
